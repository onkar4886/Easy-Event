About Us

Our motive with Easy Event is to provide students with the opportunity to easily view event details and participate in various events. The app allows students to effortlessly access event schedules, rules, prizes, and other essential information. Moreover, it assists event organizers in effectively managing registrations, payments, participant lists, and event logistics. Through Easy Event, organizing and participating in college events will be even simpler and more organized!
